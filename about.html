Who We Are

A Decade of Crafting
Extraordinary Spaces

Nestify Interiors was founded in 2013 by architects Rohan Verma and Meera Kapoor with a singular conviction: that luxury isn't defined by price tags — it's defined by intention, craftsmanship, and the feeling a space creates.

Over twelve years, we've grown from a two-person studio in Bandra to a 35-member team serving discerning homeowners across eight Indian cities. Every project we undertake is treated as a long-term relationship, not a transaction.

Our design philosophy blends global contemporary aesthetics with the warmth and sensibility of Indian living — spaces that are beautiful, functional, and unmistakably yours.
