FAQs

Quick Answers

How long does an interior project take?

A 2BHK typically takes 30–45 days, a 3BHK is 50–70 days, and larger luxury projects run 75–120 days from start to handover.

Do you work outside Mumbai?

Yes — we regularly execute projects in Pune, Thane, Navi Mumbai, and have completed work in Bengaluru, Delhi, and Hyderabad.

What is the payment schedule?

Typically 30% on project start, 40% mid-project, and 30% on completion and sign-off. Flexible schedules for large projects.

Can I live at home during renovation?

For modular kitchen and carpentry-only projects, yes. Full renovations with civil work typically require vacating for 3–6 weeks.
